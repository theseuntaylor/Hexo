# Privacy Policy for Hexo

**Last Updated: March 10, 2026**

**Hexo** ("we," "our," or "the app") is a multiplayer Tic-Tac-Toe game for Android developed by theseuntaylor. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Username/Display Name**: When you create or join a game room, you provide a player name. This name is stored in our database to facilitate gameplay.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We use **Firebase** services provided by Google, which may automatically collect:

- **Analytics Data** (via Firebase Analytics): App usage patterns, session durations, screen views, and general engagement metrics.
- **Crash Reports** (via Firebase Crashlytics): Device type, OS version, stack traces, and app state at the time of a crash.
- **Device Identifiers**: Anonymous identifiers used by Firebase to distinguish devices for analytics purposes.

### 1.3 Game Data

Game room information including board state, turn order, player connection status, room codes, and game outcomes is stored in **Google Firebase Firestore**.

### 1.4 Network Data

The app requires an **internet connection** to communicate with Firebase servers. Standard network metadata (e.g., IP address) may be logged by Firebase infrastructure.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Enable real-time multiplayer gameplay (creating rooms, joining rooms, making moves).
- Monitor app performance and fix crashes via Firebase Crashlytics.
- Understand usage patterns and improve the app experience via Firebase Analytics.
- Manage player connection status during games.

---

## 3. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do **not** sell your personal data. However, we use the following third-party services that may process data on our behalf:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| **Google Firebase Firestore** | Cloud database for game data | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| **Google Firebase Analytics** | App usage analytics | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| **Google Firebase Crashlytics** | Crash reporting | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| **Google Play Services** | Platform services | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

Data may be shared with law enforcement or governmental authorities if required by law.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Retention

- Game data (rooms, board states, usernames) is stored in **Firebase Firestore** on Google's cloud infrastructure.
- Game rooms and associated data may be retained until they are explicitly deleted or cleaned up.
- Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics data is retained according to [Google's data retention policies](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

---

## 5. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

- Communication with Firebase services over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.
- Use of Firebase's built-in security infrastructure.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Hexo is **not** specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data to us, please contact us.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Request deletion** of your data.
- **Opt out** of analytics tracking (you can reset your advertising ID or opt out of analytics in your device settings).
- **Withdraw consent** at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in the [Contact Us](#10-contact-us) section below.
